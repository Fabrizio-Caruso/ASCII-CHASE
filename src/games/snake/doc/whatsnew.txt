WHAT'S NEW 
Cross Snake 2.0

- Improved game mechanics: the snake against the wall does not prevent mines from bouncing
- 50 secrets to discover (Hint 1: collect many rings and $ items without dying; Hint 2: some horizontal walls are special)
- Achievements final screen: game ends with the number of different achievements and secrets obtained during the game
- More rings to collect with visible effects after 3, 4 and 5 rings collected without dying
- Improved levels (many more mines, some walls have been re-designed)
- SECRET and OPEN00 strings shown when a secret is found and when the secret level is unlocked
- New secret extra life item can be unlocked on bonus and secret levels
